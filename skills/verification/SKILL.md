# Verification

## Primary-Source Grounding

- Back factual claims with primary sources: official docs, source code, regulations, papers, command output, or direct artifacts.
- Popularity, summaries, and "everyone says" are not evidence.
- If a claim cannot be verified, state the gap.
- When sources conflict, surface the conflict.

## Completion Checks

Before declaring a task, loop, or goal done:

1. Re-read the objective and success criteria.
2. Map each criterion to evidence.
3. Confirm required checks actually ran.
4. List unverified assumptions or blocked checks.

Do not claim completion you have not checked.
